About

The Chemical Borrowing System was developed in the Bode group by Julian Götz.
We invite all D-CHAB groups to use the system for tracking their own chemicals!
Please inquire with Julian Götz (jgoetz@ethz.ch) or Jeffrey Bode to have your chemicals added to the system.

Features

  • All Expereact chemicals are available
  • Simple check-in/check-out on any device connected to ETHZ networks
  • Lookup of status for any bottle, helping you find the bottle you are looking for
  • Automatic updates from Expereact (move/delete)
  • Insights for superusers: Find out where all those chemicals are before the next inventory scan

FAQ

  • My phone cannot scan the QR code!
    Please use the link provided below the QR code (cbs.ethz.ch/inventory) to access the website.

  • The website does not load!
    Please check if you are connected to an ETH network. You cannot access the system from outside the ETH network.
