About

The Chemical Borrowing System was developed in the Bode group by Julian Götz.
We invite other D-CHAB groups to use the system!
Please inquire with Jeffrey Bode or Julian Götz (jgoetz@ethz.ch) to have your chemicals added to the system.

Features

  • All Expereact chemicals are available
  • Simple check-in/check-out on any device connected to ETHZ staff-net
  • Lookup of status for any bottle, helping you find the bottle you are looking for
  • Automatic updates from Expereact (move/delete)
  • Insights for superusers: Find out where all those chemicals are before the next inventory scan

FAQ

  • My phone cannot scan the QR code!
    Please use the link provided below the QR code (bode-pi2.ethz.ch/inventory) to access the website.

  • The website does not load!
    Please check if you are connected to staff-net. You cannot access the system from student-net or from outside the ETH network.

  • I'm a student, I don't have access to staff-net!
    We are sorry, you cannot use the system for security reasons. Ask your supervisor to check out the chemical for you. The system can be accessed from any device connected to staff-net.
